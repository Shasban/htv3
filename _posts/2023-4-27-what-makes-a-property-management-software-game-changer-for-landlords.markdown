# What Makes a Property Management Software Game Changer for Landlords? 


Over the decades, real estate property management practices remain the same for landlords, for example, manually messaging or calling every tenant or vendor for rent or any operations. But, what if we say you can have real-time insights into everything just with a few clicks? That’s right! Thanks to the emergence of **landlords' property management software**, having revolutionized the industry, boosting efficiency and convenience or managing properties for landlords. 
Do you wonder how? Let us explain it to you in a brief!

## How Is Property Management Software Changing Landlord’s Experience?

The demand for prop-tech i.e. software used for property management is at its peak among landlords these days due to its features streamlining everything, from accessing real-time financial reports to manual operations to analytics. In short, the entire portfolio control will be at your fingertips, making property management easier! 
Integrating this technology into your daily operations won’t just save time and money for landlords but beyond that. See how leveraging property management software becomes a game changer:

## #1: Ease transactions for you and your tenants

Collecting rent and other payments door-to-door is quite a headache for landlords since they have so much on their plate! Integrating property management software into daily operations can revolutionize manual payment and rent collection. 
For example, your tenants can make all the payments online using credit or debit cards without any delay due to its reminder and alert features while collecting deposits for maintenance charges. Contrarily, you don’t have to rush from one door to another or call for rent collection. And, it becomes easy to keep track of all the available funds and payments in the e-wallet. 
Furthermore, it's effortless to access the e-wallet just with a click! You can check the current financial status in real-time, including property distributions and contributions, payables, receivables, deposits with property snapshots, and transaction histories, like invoices and receipts.

## #2: Financial reporting

As a landlord, you will definitely agree with how challenging it is to maintain financial performance. And, to keep it on track, it’s necessary to have easy access to real-time financial reporting so you can dive deep wherever you need. 
Connecting with [property management software for landlords](https://happytenant.io/happy-landlord) allows you to take insights into on-demand reports, from rent collection to portfolio performance to receivables and payables. Also, you can leverage this software to generate detailed transaction reports, balance sheets, and P&L sorting portfolios based on vacancy status, property type, and area.   

## #3: Real-time analytics

Integrating [property management software](https://happytenant.io/blog/2023/03/22/how-to-manage-multiple-properties-with-ease), you can get transparency into portfolio operations and performance and gain visibility with graphics and charts. With its built-in features, you can work faster when comparing the financial performance of different properties based on regions and asset classes. 

## #4: Easy documentation

Gone are the days of storing piles of documents, like contracts and lease renewal papers! With property management software, you can directly access tenant contracts and financial documents from your phone. And, the best part is you will never miss the lease renewals and contract endings with its smart reminders. Moreover, you get instant updates and notifications of work order progress with attached receipts and approvals from the vendors. Hence, you don’t have to maintain a notebook manually to have all these payment and maintenance data. 

## #5: Streamline approvals

Whether it’s about approving funds for requested services, like repairs and replacements or tenant applications based on their profiles, cut the hassles of calls or messages to give your approvals! With property management software, like the HappyLandlord app, you can easily review candidate profiles and requested funds and approve directly. Moreover, you can keep track of all these approvals, fund transfers, and maintenance requests!
