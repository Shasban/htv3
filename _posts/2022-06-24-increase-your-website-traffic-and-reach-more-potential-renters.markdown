# Understand active vs. passive search


An active search is someone searching on their device on purpose – lets say for renting
Passive search is an ad that pops up in the feed based on previous behavior or profile


As property managers, you need to reach both active and passive users but make sure you are there for active users, which means you are on top of the search engines for relevant keywords.
So when a Customer is looking for a home your website appears on the search.


Your presence on social media channels hold the same importance if not more – first its aids in SEO to reach active users, plus allows you to reach passive users and build your brand presence for them for the time to come.

Listing websites are an important part of your Customer's journey. In fact, 73% of renters said they used at least one ILS site during their last property search.
