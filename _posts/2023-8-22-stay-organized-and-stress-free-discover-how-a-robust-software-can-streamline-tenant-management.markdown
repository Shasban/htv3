There’s no doubt that one of the most time-consuming and potentially stressful aspects of managing tenants is rent collection. HappyTenant offers a hassle-free solution by automating the entire rent collection process. Tenants can easily make payments online via debit or credit card in a secure environment which eliminates the need for physical checks or cash. It also records transactions so tenants can access payment history, or receipts. With secure payment gateways and automatic reminders for due dates, you can ensure timely rent payments, saving you valuable time and effort.
