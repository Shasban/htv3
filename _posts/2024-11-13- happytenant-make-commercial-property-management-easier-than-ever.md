# HappyTenant: Make Commercial Property Management Easier than Ever


Do you like commercial properties, like retail spaces, office buildings, warehouses, or malls? If yes, you know how hectic it is to manage such huge premises, from collecting rent to managing work orders to creating custom reports.


But, gone are the days of working so hard! You can manage any size of property just in a few clicks. HappyTenant is a smart property management software that eases the management of commercial properties! With its advanced features, you can automate your tasks, while having real-time performance tracking and collaboration. 


Let’s see how HappyTenant makes your commercial property management easier.


## Here’s How HappyTenant Makes Managing Commercial Properties Seamless

HappyTenant has every feature you have wished for! Leveraging all its features will allow you to track overall performance and get real-time information, which means you can manage commercial properties more efficiently. Here’s how:


### Advanced Reporting

Preparing reports manually for your commercial properties is no less than a headache! But, with HappyTenant software, you can do it in seconds by automating your property accounting. For example:

- You can keep your rent collection, banking bills, and invoices organized and on track.
- Its alert and reminder features won’t let you miss out on receivables like rent payments.
- No more handling piles of papers! You can create invoices and approval requests using your dashboard and send them to the relevant stakeholders.
- It’s easy now to configure real-time and accurate financial reports as you need and share them instantly with your landlords, property managers, and clients while exporting to any format.

### Automated Payments

What if you can do it all in one place, from managing recurring fees to rent price setup to getting paid automatically? Yes, that’s possible with HappyTenant. You don’t have to call or knock everyone for rent. Collecting deposits from landlords and rent for maintenance charges and service has become easier than ever with its online payment features. Also, its ‘Automated Late Fees’ feature will save you time and help you ensure you are receiving recurring fees on time and accurately as per your lease agreement.

### Seamless Communication

HappyTenant brings seamless and fluid communication under one integrated system. It has automated communication for every stakeholder in the line through different applications, including the Tenant app, Landlord app, Maintenance app, and Inspection app. You can stay connected with your tenants and landlords and strengthen relationships with them while gaining a competitive advantage.

In fact, there’s more you can do through this communication portal. Such as:

- Automating communication regarding lease renewals, maintenance, and document expiry updates through smart alerts and notifications
- Providing landlords instant access to financial reports, portfolio performance, and shared documents
- Greeting your customers with personalized messages on any special events or birthdays
- Easy to track and send customized reports of work orders, invoices, and statements to landlords
- Streamlining communications between vendors and owners regarding payments and approvals for service requests

### Work Order Management

With HappyTenant, you can easily manage work orders while letting your tenants have a great experience regarding property maintenance. For instance:

- Tenants can schedule and create service requests just uploading snap photos in seconds. Also, they will get instant notifications and quick responses while reviewing if the service request is assigned to vendors from anywhere, anytime.
- You can improve your response times and assign work orders easily to internal and external vendors and technicians based on their area of expertise. Besides, it’s easy to track work order progress and vendor efficiency through real-time updates and performance reports.
- Using this smart commercial property management software, landlords can stay aware of any service requests through the HappyLandlord app while getting digitally notified and requesting approvals.


Above all, HappyTenant will automate your marketing, leasing, and rental listing tasks by integrating the leading property portals into a single dashboard. You can also streamline your tenant screening process, speed up administrative work for e-leasing, and access documents from anywhere. In fact, this commercial property management software will help you make strategic decisions by providing real-time data and insights into rental portfolio performance and accessing Happiness Meter analytics to review customer satisfaction. It will help you boost your performance accordingly and make better decisions.
