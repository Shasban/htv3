# Why HappyTenant?

Allsopp & Allsopp have always put a huge emphasis on our client's experience and this was the key focus when choosing a company to partner with for our property management portfolio.

With HappyTenant the information flow to our clients is seamless. Our Landlords have everything they need to know about their investments at their fingertips and our Tenants can report any issues at the touch of a button. Internally we've advanced our efficiency levels which in turn serves to further improve our service levels to our clients.

HappyTenant aligns with our goal to lead the industry and keep up with the tech savvy world, equipping Allsopp & Allsopp with the right tools to achieve the set growth targets and expand our portfolio size.
